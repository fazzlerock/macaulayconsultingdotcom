More Joy
at Work.

A programme for teams who want to feel better about the work they do together.

Let's talk about your team
What it is

More than team-building.

Most teams don't lack goodwill. What they often lack is a shared language for talking about how they work together, and a structured way to do something about it.

More Joy at Work gives teams both. Drawing on emotional culture research, neuro-leadership frameworks and Caroline's experience working with dozens of teams across the health and public sector, the programme helps people understand what shapes their experience at work and take practical steps to improve it.

This is not a one-off team-building day. It is a considered approach to culture that meets teams where they are and helps them move, together, toward something better.

The programme

What it involves

1

Culture Assessment

The team explores how they currently feel about their work and what is shaping that experience. This draws on validated emotional culture tools and is facilitated with care and psychological safety.

2

Shared Language

The team develops a common understanding of the emotions and values that matter to them, and begins to articulate the culture they want to build.

3

Practical Action

The team identifies specific, manageable commitments they can make to one another. These are grounded in daily work, not aspirational statements.

The programme is available as a standalone engagement or as part of a broader organisational change process.
