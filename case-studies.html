Strategic Planning Facilitation · West Moreton Hospital and Health Service

Paving the way for significant change at West Moreton Health

The situation

West Moreton Hospital and Health Service was facing a period of significant organisational change and needed to engage its leadership team in a structured process that would build alignment, surface key challenges and set a clear direction for the road ahead.

What we did

Caroline designed and facilitated a series of workshops for the West Moreton leadership team, drawing on her knowledge of the Queensland health system and her expertise in structured facilitation. The process was designed to engage everyone in the room — not just senior voices — and to keep the group focused on the issues that actually mattered.

The workshops combined structured strategic conversation with practical planning tools, creating a process that was both substantive and energising.

The outcome

The leadership team achieved the clarity and alignment they needed, with a shared understanding of priorities and a practical plan for the changes ahead. The Executive Director noted that Caroline helped "pave the way for important changes ahead in our health service" and that the team "achieved the results we needed in a really positive way."
