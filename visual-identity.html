A reference document covering the colour palette, typography, design principles and components used across the website. All examples below are rendered using the live site stylesheet.

1 — Colour Palette

Deep Moss #2A3B30 Navigation, footer, testimonial sections, CTA backgrounds. The dominant dark tone — authoritative and grounded.
Moss Dark #1E2C24 Footer background. Slightly deeper than Moss for depth contrast.
Parchment #FAF7F2 Primary page background and default section background. Warm off-white with a slight cream tone.
Linen #F0EAE0 Alternating section backgrounds. Creates rhythm on long pages without sharp contrast.
Cumquat #E07828 Accent only (~10% of page). Primary buttons, italic headings, section labels, active nav underlines, list markers, phase icons. Never used as a background except on More Joy at Work hero (pale tint).
Cumquat Dark #C4681E Button hover state only.
Cumquat Pale #FDF0E4 More Joy at Work hero section background. The only cumquat-family background on the site.
Stone #DDD5C8 Borders, dividers, large decorative numerals. The workhorse neutral.
Slate #5C5248 Secondary text — form labels, section metadata, supporting copy.
Charcoal #1E1A16 Primary text colour for headings and body copy.

Proportion rule: Parchment and Linen form the page canvas (~60%). Moss is used heavily for dark sections (~25%). Cumquat appears as an accent only — never dominant — to maintain warmth without overwhelming the calm, authoritative tone (~10%). Stone and Slate are supporting neutrals.

3 — Design Principles

01 Flat, not cold

No drop shadows, no gradients. Depth comes from background colour alternation (parchment/linen/moss) and subtle 1px stone borders. The page feels warm, not corporate.

02 Restrained accent

Cumquat appears sparingly — buttons, underlines, square markers, em tags in headings. When it appears, it draws the eye. If it appeared everywhere, it would draw the eye nowhere.

03 Editorial rhythm

Large section-label type, generous white space, and Cormorant's high contrast strokes create a magazine-like quality. The site reads, not just scans.

04 Micro-interactions

Nav links reveal a cumquat underline on hover via scaleX(0→1). Workshop cards rotate slightly and lift on hover. Arrow links widen their gap. Buttons shift 1px up. All at 0.2s ease.

05 Section rhythm

Pages alternate: parchment → linen → parchment for content sections, with moss reserved exclusively for testimonials and CTAs. This creates predictable warmth at structural moments.

06 Scroll reveal

Elements fade and rise 22px into position as they enter the viewport (0.6s ease). Staggered via delay classes (0.1s, 0.2s, 0.3s, 0.4s). Disabled for reduced-motion preference.
