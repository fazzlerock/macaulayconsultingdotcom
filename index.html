Caroline Macaulay partners with boards, leaders and teams who are doing important work but have lost — or never quite found — their footing. Whether the challenge is strategy, culture or change, she brings the structure, warmth and sector knowledge to move things forward.

Working across Queensland's health, community and public sector for over a decade, Caroline has helped organisations as large as Queensland Health and as focused as a local community service find their direction and build the internal confidence to act on it.

Why it works

What happens in a great workshop?

The best ones don't just produce outputs — they change how people see the work, each other, and what's possible.

1

People feel heard

Every voice in the room is drawn out. Competing perspectives become assets rather than obstacles.

2

Complexity becomes clarity

The messy, tangled situation that walked in the door gets named, mapped and made workable.

3

Ideas become action

Energy and insight get translated into real priorities and practical next steps people actually commit to.

4

Momentum emerges

People leave with renewed direction and a shared sense of what they're building together.
